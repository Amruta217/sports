Rules

  1. Game comprise of at least one innings where each team will take turns on batting and fielding/bowling.
  2. Cricket is played between two teams with eleven players on each side.
  3. The fielding team will have a bowler who bowls to the batsman who tries to hit the ball with the bat.
  4. The batsmen tries to score as many runs as possible before getting out. Some of the ways to score runs can be found here.
  5. The fielding team tries to restrict the batting team by getting them out. Some of the ways a batsmen can get out is found here.
